About Us

Homlei is an affordable move-in ready home. We believe that peace of mind, comfort and support are the foundation to achieving your dreams. That’s why we manage one of the most livable accommodation for students as a stepping stone from boarding, to building a lifestyle of your own.

Living local

MELBOURNE CBD

Melbourne has been a long time ‘most liveable city’ that bustles with excitement and activity. Melbourne is a world leading business and academic centre, an ideal place for inspiring the next generation of world leaders. But it can’t be all work and no play, Melbourne has a vibrant culture with spectacular art and festivals including a labyrinth of exciting laneways full interesting places to see and hang-out.
The local food and café culture is the envy of cities around Australia and the world, wake up and wherever you are you’ll be a short walk from plenty of great coffee and brunch shops.
On your weekends take ride through the parklands, along the river, or head to the MCG or Tennis Centre for a never-ending calendar of epic sporting events. Numerous bars run all day and night with live music, DJ’s.
